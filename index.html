PhD Student, Mechanical Engineering · Purdue University

I research cryogenic boiling and two-phase flow & heat transfer at the Boiling and Two-Phase Flow Laboratory (BTPFL), advised by Professor Issam Mudawar. I am currently working on three projects:

  1. Characterization of liquid nitrogen spray using different nozzles with a PDPA system
  2. Pool boiling and spray cooling using liquid nitrogen under Earth and microgravity
  3. Physics-assisted machine learning techniques for predicting flow boiling HTC and CHF for Earth and microgravity

Before Purdue: B.E. Mechanical Engineering at NUST (GPA 3.92/4.0, minor in AI), with research stints at Queen’s University and Sabanci University.
