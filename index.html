I am a Graduate Research Assistant at the Boiling and Two-Phase Flow Laboratory (BTPFL) at Purdue University, advised by Professor Issam Mudawar. My research focuses on cryogenic boiling under different gravity conditions and two-phase flow and heat transfer.

Before Purdue, I earned my B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from NUST, Pakistan (GPA 3.92/4.0) with a minor in Artificial Intelligence, and held research positions at Queen’s University (Canada) and Sabanci University (Türkiye).
